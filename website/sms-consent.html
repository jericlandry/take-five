SMS Consent

Take Five Care · takefive.care

When a family administrator adds you to a Take Five care circle, you may provide your mobile number to receive SMS notifications from Take Five Care. This page describes what you are agreeing to and how to opt out at any time.

Take Five Care sends SMS messages only to people who have been added to a care circle by a family administrator and have explicitly consented to receive messages. We do not send marketing messages.

What messages you'll receive

Take Five is primarily an inbound service — you text us, not the other way around. The only messages you will receive from Take Five Care are:

Welcome message — a one-time message when you are enrolled, confirming the number to text and how the service works.

Check-in confirmations — a brief automated reply each time you send a care update, confirming your message was received by the care circle.

We will not send you unsolicited updates, alerts, or marketing messages.

Opt in to SMS notifications

If your family administrator has asked you to provide your number, complete the form below to confirm your consent.

Mobile opt-in data and consent will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message. To get help, reply HELP. For support, visit takefive.care.

Thank you — your consent has been recorded. You'll receive a welcome message from Take Five Care shortly.

Opting out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from Take Five Care. You will receive one final confirmation that you have been unsubscribed. To re-subscribe, reply START.

For help, reply HELP to any message or visit takefive.care.
